Refund Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

1. Digital Product Policy

Due to the immediate access provided to digital assets (such as the Core Prompt Toolkit, recorded sessions, and proprietary workflows), all one-time payments for the Starter and Professional tiers are final and non-refundable once the course materials have been accessed.

2. VIP Executive and Consulting Services

For VIP Executive packages and custom team onboarding, any requests for cancellation must be made prior to the execution of the custom automation audit and 1-on-1 strategy sessions. Once consulting services have commenced, the fee is entirely non-refundable.

3. Exceptional Circumstances

If you experience technical issues preventing you from accessing the course platform, please contact our support team within 7 days of purchase. We will work to resolve the issue promptly or offer an appropriate remedy.
