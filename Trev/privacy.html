Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you enroll, we collect personal information necessary to deliver the course, including your name, email address, phone number, and professional field. Payment details are processed securely by our third-party payment gateways; we do not store your credit card or bank information on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To grant access to the learning platform and issue verifiable digital certificates.
  • To add you to private community channels and live coaching sessions.
  • To send you course updates, module releases, and relevant promotional materials.

3. Data Sharing

We respect your privacy. Your personal information will never be sold, rented, or shared with third-party marketers. For VIP Executive clients, team data gathered during custom automation audits remains strictly confidential and is used solely for strategy formulation.
