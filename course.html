Syllabus Breakdown

7 Core Sessions

A structured, linear learning path designed to transition you from an explorer into a confident AI operator.

Get every account set up and learn the fundamentals of how these two flagship models actually work.

  • Setting up ChatGPT and Gemini accounts (free tier only, no paid tools required)
  • How LLMs generate responses, and where each tool's strengths differ
  • Your first structured prompts: moving from casual questions to clear instructions

Move past one-line questions and start programming the model to give you the output you actually want.

  • Role prompting: giving the AI a persona to sharpen its answers
  • Being specific about format, length, and audience
  • Comparing ChatGPT and Claude on the same task to see where each excels

Refine your prompting further, then learn which tool to reach for depending on the task in front of you.

  • Iterating on a draft instead of accepting the first answer
  • Step-by-step reasoning prompts for anything analytical
  • Building a personal decision tree: which AI tool for which job

Learn to research and synthesize information rather than just generate it.

  • Running NotebookLM to turn your own notes and documents into a queryable assistant
  • Using Perplexity for sourced, up-to-date research
  • Cross-referencing multiple sources cleanly and verifying claims

A hands-on tour of the most common ways people put AI to work day-to-day.

  • Writing, editing, and brainstorming
  • Planning, organizing, and summarizing
  • The remaining core use cases, applied to your own goals

Connect multiple tools into a single workflow, and think critically about responsible use.

  • Passing output from one AI tool into another as part of one workflow
  • Where AI gets things wrong, and how to catch it
  • Using AI responsibly: attribution, privacy, and knowing the tool's limits

Present the project you've built across the course, and receive your Certificate of Completion.

  • Presenting your final project to the group
  • Peer and instructor feedback
  • Certificate of Completion (requires attending at least 5 of 7 sessions)
