Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you join the waitlist or register, we may collect your name, email address, WhatsApp number, country, selected package, registration reference, and the transfer reference or sender name you provide for payment matching. When you use the student portal, we may also process assignment files, submission notes, timestamps, review status, and instructor feedback. We do not ask for or store your card PIN, bank password, or online-banking credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To match a bank transfer with a registration and manually approve enrollment.
  • To generate and manage a personal student-portal access code.
  • To send registration, approval, class, support, and course-resource messages by email or WhatsApp.
  • To deliver package-specific materials, receive and review assignments, provide feedback, manage community access, coaching, and certificates.

3. Storage and Service Providers

Registration records, resource settings, and submission records are stored in Google Sheets and processed through Google Apps Script. Assignment files are stored in a private Google Drive folder controlled by TREV AI. Approval and submission emails are sent through the Google account operating the system, and WhatsApp may be used for manual support and code delivery. These providers process data under their own privacy and security terms.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal information to third-party marketers. Information is shared only when needed to operate enrollment, learning access, communications, or when required by law. For VIP Executive clients, information gathered during automation audits is used only for the agreed service.

5. Access Codes and Device Storage

If a student selects “Remember this code,” the access code is stored in that browser's local storage on that device. Students should not use this option on a shared or public device. Portal verification events may record the last access time and login count for support and misuse prevention.

6. Your Choices

You may request correction or deletion of your registration information by emailing trevaisupport01@gmail.com, subject to records we must retain for payment, legal, fraud-prevention, or course-administration purposes.
